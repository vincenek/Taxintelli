Bank Payment

Pay your taxes directly at any commercial bank:

  1. Visit any commercial bank with your Tax Identification Number (TIN)
  2. Request a tax payment slip
  3. Fill in your details and the tax amount
  4. Make payment at the counter
  5. Collect your payment receipt

Note: Keep your payment receipt as proof of payment for at least 6 years.

Online Payment

Pay your taxes securely through the FIRS e-TaxPay platform:

  1. Visit the FIRS e-TaxPay portal (https://etax.firs.gov.ng)
  2. Login with your TIN and password
  3. Select the tax type and assessment year
  4. Enter the payment amount
  5. Choose your payment method (card, bank transfer, etc.)
  6. Complete the payment and download your receipt

Note: Online payments are processed instantly and receipts are available immediately.

POS Payment

Pay your taxes at designated POS locations:

  1. Visit any FIRS-approved POS location
  2. Provide your TIN and tax details
  3. Make payment with your debit card
  4. Collect your payment receipt

Note: POS payments are convenient for small to medium tax amounts and are available in urban centers across Nigeria.
